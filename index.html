Founder

Ankit Mishra

Founder & Principal · Forbes Contributor

Ankit Mishra is a commercial strategist and AI transformation leader specialising in African fintech and cleantech. He is Founder and Principal of AM Consulting Group and a Forbes contributor with 50+ published articles reaching 200,000+ readers.

Ankit is a Member of the AI & Trust Working Group at the Schwartz Reisman Institute, University of Toronto — where he built the AI Trajectory Index, scoring 186 economies on AI readiness alongside researchers from Harvard, BCG, and Meta. He is Entrepreneur-in-Residence at Ivey Business School and Mentor at McGill's TechAccel accelerator. Previously, he led commercial expansion at Pngme across five African markets during its $15M Series A, and served as Economist at the OECD and IEA in Paris.
